Project information

  • Category: Mobile App
  • Project date: 20 May, 2022
  • Project URL: https://ontrak-capstone.herokuapp.com/

OnTrak is a habit-tracking app developed primarily for mobile use. Created in conjunction with 4 other developers, I assisted in leadership in the role of Scrum Master and organized an verifiably efficient and productive sprint (see my feedback in #About). With my team, we sought to learn and deploy new concepts such as image-hosting with Firebase, data visualization with (Vue)Chart.js, and custom asset design through our badge award system. I was also responsible for the algorithm behind our line-graph, which looks backwards through time.

OnTrak was developed using Vue3 for the front-end and Node.JS for the back-end. The project involved significant coordination using version-control through Git, and intensive troubleshooting when deploying using Firebase.
